Common questions

No. It uses natural voice and is trained on your business's actual website language — services, hours, tone. It answers like a knowledgeable team member, not like a script.

It only answers from what's on your website. If it doesn't know something, it says so and offers to take the visitor's name and number for you to follow up. It never invents prices or makes promises.
